SYNLawn DIY Installation Instructions

Are you ready to give installing your own SYNLawn a go? We are here to help. Whether you are doing this yourself or are hiring a landscaper to help you, please be advised that while the installation isn't rocket science, it can be very labor intensive. We highly recommend that this be a DIY project only if there aren't seams involved as glue and seam tape management can prove to be tricky the first time around. However, our helpful guide and videos can help you through this process as well.

Also keep in mind that preparing your base for proper drainage is just as important as laying your turf. Depending on the size of your project, many tons of aggregate or base rock will need to be delivered to your property and dumped on your driveway or street and will need to be wheelbarrowed to your project area. If you are still determined after knowing all of this, check out our installation guide and detail drawing on this page and review our videos as well.

Once you are prepared, give us a call and we will be happy to process your material order and deliver to your project location.
